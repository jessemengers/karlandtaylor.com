Karl & Taylor

The Barn at the Woods in Edmond, Oklahoma

Our Story

"You know you're in love when you can't fall asleep because reality is finally better than your dreams." - Dr. Seuss

  • 2011 September

    They meet at track team tryouts

    Karl and Taylor met at Southwestern Christian University while Taylor was trying out for the track team. He was already on the team from the year before and a mutual friend of theirs introduced them.

  • 2012 April

    First comes comradery then comes romance

    They quickly became friends seeing each other at practice everyday. After a few months they started talking and spending time together outside of practice. On April 28th 2012, they officially began dating.

  • 2016 June

    Picnic proposal

    Taylor's family was visiting in Oklahoma City and were meeting Karl's family for a picnic at the park where Karl and Taylor used to have track practice. Family and friends had arrived and all were having a great time, when Taylor's father asked her and Karl to go to the spot where they had first met for a photo. Right after he took the picture, Karl got down on one knee and asked Taylor to spend the rest of her life with him and she said YES!

  • 2017 November

    Time for wedding bells

    On November 3rd, Karl and Taylor will exchange vows and join together in matrimony. Their family and friends will be there to celebrate this special union.
